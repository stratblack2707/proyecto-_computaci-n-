Estas son las preguntas que escribe para la entrevista

Interviewer: Hi! How are you? My name is Jose Luis and I am estudent from CECyT 4. I'm very interested in learning about different cultures and perspectives, and I would like to interview you. Do you mind if I record our conversation?

Interviewee: Hello! Nice to meet you. I don't mind if you record the interview.
Interviewer: Great! Thank you for your cooperation. Now, let me present you with some questions to learn more about you and your experience. I'm excited to hear your answers.
[Questions]
What is your full name and where are you from?
What is your nationality, and how long have you been living in this country?
What brought you to Mexico, and what has been your experience so far?
Could you tell us a bit about your native culture and the main cultural differences you have encountered here?
What aspects of Mexico culture do you find most interesting or fascinating?
Have you faced any challenges or difficulties in adapting to life in [country]? If so, how have you overcome them?
Is there anything else you would like to share about your experience as a foreigner in [country]?
[Closing] Interviewer: Thank you so much for sharing your experiences and perspectives

"Este es mi sentir a la hora de hacer mi entrevista"

Al principio tuve miedo de no encontrar a ningun extranjero, porque conforme avanzabamos por las calles solo veiamos a mexicanos pero decidimos ir al centro de Coyoacan y entonces fuè cuando los encontramos. Fue dificil encontrar a alguien que quisiera ser grabbado, unos se negaban o incluso otros ni siquiera te respondian. Al final encontre a una Italiana que sabia hablar ingles y muy amablemente me concedio grabarla y hacerle unas cuantas preguntas.
